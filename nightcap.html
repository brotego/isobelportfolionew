Gitane Nightcap

NEW YORK, NY Co-Host
December 15, 2023 • Design by Isobel Lola
January 20, 2024 • Design by Isobel Lola
NIGHTCAP brings together downtown's creative youth for intimate monthly events at Cafe Gitane, where rotating hosts and DJs shape each night. The series transforms the beloved French-Moroccan institution into an after-hours gathering spot for artists and young people. These evenings blend music and conversation in a space that's become a cornerstone of Mott Street culture.
